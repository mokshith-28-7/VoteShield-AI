Process

How VoteShield works

A seamless four-step journey from registration to certified results.

01
📋

Register & Verify

Voters register with official ID credentials. The system verifies eligibility and activates the account through secure OTP confirmation.

02
🗳️

Authenticate & Vote

On election day, voters log in with MFA, view candidate info, and securely cast their encrypted vote with a single confirmed action.

03
🤖

AI Monitors & Validates

Every vote triggers an AI risk-scoring check. Suspicious patterns are flagged in real-time; clean votes are immediately counted.

04
📊

Results & Reports

Voting closes, final tallies are verified, and certified results with full audit trails and AI risk reports are published instantly.
